Hills of Steel is an action shooter game which puts you behind the wheel of a tank! Race and shoot your way through a hilled battlefield filled with enemies. There's tons of tanks, mechs and robots to unlock and use in your fight! You can also use powerful Boosters like mines, force shields and rocket strikes to make your tank even more dangerous! Can you build the ultimate tank and conquer the Hills of Steel?

How to play Hills of Steel?

  • Move: use the A and D keys or the Arrow keys to drive your tank!
  • Shoot: use the spacebar to shoot!
  • Repair: press the B button to repair your tank!
